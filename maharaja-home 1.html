The Venue

One address for the whole celebration.

Maharaja Guruvayoor is a wedding and convention centre a short distance from the Sree Krishna Temple. Its Darbar Hall — the first restored space of the complex — seats up to five hundred guests across a ground floor, auditorium level and balcony, with dining for two hundred and fifty. A ceremony can move from muhurtham to reception without ever leaving the building.

Emerald and brass, warm wood and unhurried light — one material language throughout, so the room feels considered rather than staged.

Location

Minutes from the temple.

For a Guruvayoor wedding, nearness to the Sree Krishna Temple is not a convenience — it is the reason families choose the town. Maharaja Guruvayoor sits close to the temple and just off the main road, with easy approach for guests arriving from across Kerala.
